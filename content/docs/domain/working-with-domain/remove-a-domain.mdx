---
title: Removing a Domain from a Project on Brimble
---

Learn how to seamlessly remove a domain from your project and your Brimble account with this step-by-step guide.

## Navigate to the Domains Tab

To begin, navigate to the Domains tab within the Project Overview section of your Brimble dashboard. Locate the domain you wish to remove and click on the "Edit" button associated with it.

## Click the Remove Button

Upon clicking the "Edit" button, a set of options will be displayed. Look for the "Remove" button and click on it to initiate the removal process for the domain from the project.

## Remove Domain from Your Account

If you also want to remove the domain from all projects associated with your account, proceed to the Domains section of your dashboard. Here, you'll find a list of all domains under your account. Locate the specific domain you wish to remove, and from the context menu, select the "Delete" option.

By following these steps, you can efficiently remove a domain from your Brimble project and your account, ensuring a streamlined and organized experience.
