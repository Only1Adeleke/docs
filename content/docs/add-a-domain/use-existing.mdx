Brimble enables you to integrate your existing domain with ease, allowing you to maintain your established online presence while leveraging the power of our platform. Whether you're migrating an existing project or building something new, our intuitive process ensures seamless integration for developers like you.

import { Step, Steps } from 'fumadocs-ui/components/steps';

<Steps>

<Step>

### Navigate to Your Project's Domain Settings

Begin by selecting your project from the **Brimble Dashboard**. Once inside, navigate to the **Settings** tab and locate the **Domains** menu item.  
Clicking this option will take you to the domain settings page, where you can manage all domain-related configurations for your project.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1716305302/Screenshot_96_xh38tm.png)

</Step>

<Step>

### Add Your Existing Domain to Your Project

On the **Domains** page, you'll find a field designed for adding a custom domain. Simply enter the domain name you want to integrate with your project and click **Add Domain**.  
This step prepares your domain for connection with Brimble's robust infrastructure, paving the way for seamless integration.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1716305302/Screenshot_96_xh38tm.png)

</Step>

<Step>

### Configure Your DNS Records

To complete the integration, you need to update your domain's DNS settings with your domain registrar.  
Brimble provides clear and detailed instructions, including the required DNS records (e.g., A, CNAME, or TXT) to point your domain to our servers.

Refer to the **Brimble Dashboard** for step-by-step guidance tailored to your domain registrar. Once configured, your domain will be linked seamlessly to your project.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dfdilofw7/image/upload/v1716308458/Screenshot_99_y5jdru.png)

</Step>

</Steps>

### Key Features of Domain Integration with Brimble:
- **Customizable**: Maintain full control over your domain settings while leveraging Brimble's infrastructure.
- **Flexibility**: Works with any domain registrar, ensuring compatibility and ease of use.
- **Seamless Deployment**: Automatic setup ensures your domain integrates smoothly with your project.

With Brimble, integrating your custom domain is as simple as following these steps. Build, scale, and deploy with confidence while maintaining your unique online identity.
